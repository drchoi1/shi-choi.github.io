Hotel booking

Our wedding will take place at the beautiful Mia Nha Trang Resort. To make your stay as convenient as possible, we have secured a special discounted rate for a variety of room types, with breakfast included.

Please review the room options below, along with the resort website, and send your completed reservation form to sm@mianhatrang.com by February 3, 2027.

February 3 is the deadline for this special pricing. However, rooms may sell out before then, so we recommend making your reservation as soon as you have purchased your flight tickets.

FYI, VND 1 million is approximately USD 39 (40 if you want to be conservative and make your math easier). Or here's an online converter.
