Hotel booking

We would love for you to stay with us at Mia Nha Trang Resort, where the wedding celebration will take place.

To make planning easier, we have arranged a special room block with several room and villa options. Please review the options below, then send your completed reservation form to sm@mianhatrang.com by February 3, 2027.

Rooms may sell out before the deadline, so we recommend booking once your travel plans are set.

For a quick estimate, VND 1 million is approximately USD 39. You can also use this currency converter.

Room block details

  • Rates are net per room, per night.
  • Rates include 5% service charge and 10% VAT.
  • Breakfast is included for the number of adults listed on each room option.
  • Each bedroom also includes breakfast for 1 child under 6 sharing with parents.
  • Breakfast for children 6-11 is VND 578,000 per child, per day when sharing with parents.
  • An extra bed is VND 1,500,000 per day and includes breakfast.
  • Children older than 11 count as adults.
  • The special pricing deadline is February 3, 2027, subject to availability.
